Frequently Asked Question:

  • How do I look after the product?

    • You can look after our product in category (using the links in the menu to find what you are looking for)

  • How do I order some items?

    • You can click in product category, and fill our form.

  • Can I change my shipping address once my order has been dispatched?

    • We are unfortunately unable to redirect orders once your items have been dispatched. Therefore, please ensure you provide a suitable shipping address for the specified delivery items.

  • Can I place an order over the telephone?

    • Of course you can order our product over the telephone. Contact our Customer Care team +65-44446677 or you can email frias@gmail.com for advice on how to place your order. -

  • Can I return an item to one of the FRIAS store?

    • No, items cannot be returned to one of the stores. Please ensure you post your return to: 100, Orchard Crescent, 100000 Singapore.
